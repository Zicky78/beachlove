About


Beautiful Beach Stones, Holey Stones, Beach Pottery, Sea Glass, Ocean Sandstone, Ocean Jasper, and other treasured gifts from the sea. All stones are handpicked from Pacific Coast beaches and authentic 100% tumbled by the sea.




HOLEY STONES- (aka Lucky Stones/Hag Stones/Talisman Stones) I love collecting these unique stones, when I'm lucky enough to find one. The holes and grooves in these stones are naturally formed when small mollusks called Piddock (aka Angel Wings) drill a hole in the stone using their shell and then they live in the hole. If the hole goes all the way through, the stone is long believed to protect against evil and possess magical powers. Some of these stones still have remnants of shell or tiny pebbles embedded in the holes.




BEACH POTTERY- These beautiful rustic beach pottery shards are so much fun to find. Pottery was widely used and discarded in the oceans during the 18th and 19th century. It's always exciting to find old Indian pottery shards too.




ESSENTIAL OIL DIFFUSERS- Porous beach stones make great essential oil diffusers. Just dab a little on your stone or pottery shard for a long lasting scent.
